Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics from Universidade Federal da Paraíba (2004), master's degree in Mathematics from Universidade Federal de Campina Grande (2006), PhD in Mathematics from Programa de Doutorado Associado UFPB / UFCG (2013). with a postdoctoral degree, in Functional Analysis, at the Universidade Federal de Uberlândia (2014). He is currently associate professor II at the Universidade Federal da Paraíba - Campus IV and professor of the Postgraduate Program in Mathematics at UFPB. He has experience in Mathematics, with emphasis on Functional Analysis, working mainly on the following topics: linear and nonlinear theory of operators and polynomials between Banach spaces, operators and polynomial ideals, Banach space theory.
